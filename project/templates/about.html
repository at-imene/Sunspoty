What is Sunspoty?

Sunspoty is a web-based application designed to assist in the analysis and understanding of sunspots. It provides two main features:

  • Automatic Calculation of Wolf Number

    Using advanced algorithms, Sunspoty can analyze an image of the sun and automatically calculate the Wolf number. The Wolf number is a widely used parameter for quantifying the number and complexity of sunspots, providing valuable information for solar activity monitoring.

  • Sunspot Classification

    Sunspoty offers a sunspot classification feature that allows users to upload a magnetogram image of a sunspot. Based on the uploaded image, Sunspoty utilizes machine learning techniques to classify the sunspot into one of three categories: alpha, beta, or betax. This classification helps researchers and enthusiasts gain insights into the characteristics and behavior of sunspots.
